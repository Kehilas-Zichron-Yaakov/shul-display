Zmanim


It is good to go lechumra on the zmanim by 1-2 minutes if possible.

Zmanim are calculated using a javascript implementation of the KosherJava Zmanim library. The zmanim are calculated based on the shul's latitude, longitude, and elevation; which is 41.137804, -74.044059, 169 meters respectively.
